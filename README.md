SLS Verify 🛡️🇳🇬

Helping Nigerians understand telecom and banking security issues  while reducing customer support stress for fintech platforms.

SLS Verify is a telecom-financial troubleshooting and risk-awareness platform designed to help users understand common issues like:

- OTP delays
- SIM swap restrictions
- blocked transfers
- login failures
- telecom related banking security holds.

As telecom and financial systems become more connected, users often panic when transactions fail or OTPs stop arriving. This creates confusion, fraud risk, and heavy customer support traffic for banks and fintech companies.

SLS Verify aims to simplify these situations through guided troubleshooting and security awareness.



💡 What SLS Verify Does

SLS Verify helps users:

- understand possible causes of transaction/security issues
- receive safe next-step guidance
- learn about SIM-related banking restrictions
- reduce confusion during telecom financial security events.

Example issues:

- “Why am I not receiving OTP?”
- “Why was my transfer blocked after SIM replacement?”
- “Could this be a security hold?”

⚖️ Privacy & Safety

SLS Verify does NOT request:

- OTPs
- passwords
- ATM PINs
- sensitive banking credentials

This platform is intended for educational guidance and troubleshooting assistance only.
